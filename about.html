About Us

Best Medical Care For Yourself and Your Family

Kenyan hospitals have always experienced a problem with congestion. This affects the timely access to emergency healthcare services leading to life-threatening delays for time-sensitive injuries and medical emergencies. We face major challenges by having to go to hospitals to book appointments or going without an appointment booked. A lot of time is wasted when queueing in hospitals. As a result of the long queues, there is risk of infection due to congestion and possibility of air borne infections. And lastly serious medical emergencies may not be attended to early enough. There are several systems that have been created to deal with these problems but they also have their own setbacks. SAAS mHealth Kenya for example is one of the systems available but it is not freely available on the net. MyHealth Africa is another one of these systems and its setback is that its services are not fully available in some countries.

Our aim is to enable the patient to log in and view various doctors and the departments they work in and therefore book an appointment with their preferred doctor for a specific date and time.
